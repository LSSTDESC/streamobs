DES#

DES is supported by StreamObs.

Available releases#

DES Y6 Gold#

We have added the DES Y6 Gold as a supported survey to use with streamobs. The survey dataset is described in Bechtol et al. 2025, and the catalogs can are documented/publically available from DESDM. The maglim, completeness, and photoerror files should be downloaded and placed in the data/surveys/des_yr6/ folder and loaded in the following manner:

des_yr6 = surveys.Survey.load(survey='des', release='yr6')

Any questions about the creation of these survey specific files can be addressed to Peter Ferguson.
